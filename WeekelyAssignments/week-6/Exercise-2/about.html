International Scholarships for Nepalese Students in Australia

Being one of the most popular study destinations in the world, Australia offers multiple scholarships for Nepalese students to study in Australia. Their education system is globally renowned and is highly regarded as one of the best in the world. To further extend their support to international students, the Government of Australia has in fact invested over AUD$ 300 million for scholarships for international students in Australia. International students may apply for different types of scholarships in some of the best universities in Australia. Scholarships are awarded primarily on the basis of merit, and applicants must show their qualifications for the scholarship they are applying for. There are plenty of scholarships in Australia for Nepalese students seeking financial support to pursue their study abroad goals.
